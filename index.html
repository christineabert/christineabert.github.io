How I work

A process built around your problem, not a template.

Every engagement is different. But the thinking behind it isn't — I start with understanding before I start designing. Design is a team sport, and I play best when I'm embedded, not outsourced.

01
Discover & Research
I listen before I sketch.
User & stakeholder interviews · Vision mapping · Product value proposition · Competitive analysis
02
Define & Frame
Clarity before creativity.
User personas · Empathy mapping · Problem statements · User journey mapping · Jobs to be done
03
Ideate & Prototype
Ideas are cheap. Tangible beats theoretical.
Design sprints · Wireframing · Interactive prototypes · Service blueprinting · Co-creation workshops
04
Test & Iterate
Assumptions are expensive. Testing isn't.
Usability testing · UX & accessibility audit · A/B experiments · Expert review · Moderated & unmoderated studies
05
Deliver & Handoff
Designed to be built, not just admired.
Pixel-perfect specs · Design system · Developer handoff · Annotation & documentation · QA support

About me

Design starts before Figma.

With 15 years in Product Design and UX, my core focus is healthcare - but I've also worked across automotive, industrial and factory environments, which has sharpened my ability to design for complex workflows and specialist users.

I believe great design means connecting the dots between business, brand, product and service. And it starts long before prompting in Claude Code or pushing pixels in Figma - it starts with talking to people and actually understanding them.

If you need a designer who gets up to speed fast and drives the process with sharpness and craft - that's where I do my best work.

How we would work together

Open communication, visible progress from day one.

I work as a collaborative partner, not a vendor. Whether you need focused momentum on a specific challenge or a steady design presence over time - I adapt to where you are and what you actually need.

Product Discovery Workshop

Know where you stand before you decide where to go.

You have a product, first ideas and open questions - but no clear path forward yet. In a structured workshop we align on what you know, review what exists, and define concrete next steps together.

  • Align on business goals and define your north star
  • Review existing product experience and identify pain points
  • Structure and prioritise ideas - quick wins vs. strategic features
  • An outside perspective that sees what internal teams often miss
  • Concrete next steps as output - backlog or roadmap-ready
Design Partner

Embedded, not outsourced.

A longer engagement where I work as part of your team - driving design across discovery, product and brand. I bring senior design leadership without the overhead of a permanent hire.

  • Senior design leadership scaled to your context
  • Trusted sparring partner for product decisions
  • Fluent in complex stakeholder environments
  • Fast to context, faster to value
  • I integrate into your team fast - no lengthy onboarding needed
